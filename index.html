Welcome to Jon's Portfolio

Glass App

A client website to showcase physical products in a niche technology field. The admin has a secret login page and when logged in, they have access to full CRUD. Integrated S3 buckets for uploading and pulling objects, as well as automated e-mail system to send messages sent by users from the contact us page.

Custom Shirts

Create custom t-shirt designs, where the user can upload their own logos, pick any color t-shirt they want, and write text of their choice. Instead of discussing a users needs over the phone with the t-shirt printer, the user can use this sandbox to create their perfect product.

Hand Painted

Jon created Hand Painted as a forum for art enthusiasts who want to take their art with them wherever they go. We are on our laptops so often that it seems fitting we'd express ourselves creatively through our laptop covers.

GitPub

Digital Closet

Add clothes to your digital closet to easily see what an outfit will look like by clicking on the clothing item that you would like to add.
